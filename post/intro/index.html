Good Morning ...

2019/08/19

Hello there; thank you for stopping by. In this post, I intend to answer the question, “What is this, and why does it exist?”

I’m setting up this blog as a space for me to “learn in public”. I intend for it to serve as a quasi-journal into which I distill the different things that I’ll be learning, working on, and thinkig about. I expect the focus of the posts to be on two main areas: Programming and product.

Programming posts will feature computer science topics that, in my experience and opinion, are foundational. There exists quite a few programming blogs on the web. Some of them are even good! I intend for my programming posts to be among the good ones by emphasizing complete and understandable exposition of the topic in question.

Product (working title) posts will explore how existing consumer products could be transformed or improved in order to serve, what I believe to be, overlooked market segments. Some posts under this banner will be of product ideas that I’m currently thinking about. As with the programming posts, the aim with product posts will be presenting the ideas in a detailed fashion. As such, I anticipate these posts to resemble product design documents, but may sometimes end up being public logs of ill-conceived ideas.
